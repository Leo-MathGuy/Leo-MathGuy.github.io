Rick Astley

The undying internet meme

Rick Astley was a singer that sang a song that many have heard,

Never Gonna Give You Up
  • 1966: Rick Astley is born on February 6, 1966, in Newton-le-Willows, England.
  • 1985: In 1985, Astley joins the music industry as a recording artist.
  • 1987: His debut single "Never Gonna Give You Up" is released in 1987.
  • 1987: The song becomes a massive hit, reaching No. 1 on the charts.
  • 1987: "Whenever You Need Somebody," his debut album, releases in 1987.
  • 1988: Rick Astley wins the Best Newcomer Award at the Brit Awards in 1988.
  • 1990s: Rick Astley takes a break from music in the early '90s.
  • 2007: The Rickroll phenomenon begins around 2007 on the internet.
  • 2007: "Rickrolling" involves users being tricked into clicking a link to the "Never Gonna Give You Up" music video.
  • 2008: Rick Astley embraces the Rickroll and even Rickrolls the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade in 2008.
  • 2016: Rick Astley releases new music in the 2010s, including "50" in 2016.
  • 2010s: He tours globally, delighting fans with his live performances.
  • Today: Rick Astley's songs continue to be used in various forms of media and pop culture.
  • Internet Era: He becomes a beloved figure on the internet due to the Rickroll.
  • Decades: Rick Astley's career spans several decades, with enduring popularity.
  • Present: He maintains a strong presence on social media, engaging with fans.
  • Internet Culture: Rick Astley's music and the Rickroll meme remain iconic parts of internet culture.
